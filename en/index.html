About Us

40 Years of Experience
at Industry's Service

Evolution Engineering & Service was founded on the union of technical and managerial expertise built over more than 40 years in heavy steel fabrication, industrial piping, the naval sector and industrial insulation.

Our production facility in Crispiano (TA) covers approximately 3,000 m² indoors and over 25,000 m² of equipped open yards, with state-of-the-art machinery capable of handling technically complex orders.

We operate across heavy industry, energy, naval and infrastructure sectors, serving leading clients at national and international level.

What We Do

Our Services

From design to delivery: a complete offer for the heavy metalworking industry.

Heavy Steel Fabrication

Design and construction of complex steel structures, pressure vessels, tanks, hoppers, towers, lattice frames and special fabrications in compliance with current standards.

Industrial Piping

Fabrication, supply and installation of piping networks in carbon steel, stainless, duplex and HDPE for process, energy and hospital plants. Certified TIG and SAW welding with radiographic inspection.

Naval Sector

Specialist labour supply and fabrication of structural components for shipbuilding and port works, with experience on contracts for the Italian Navy and major national shipyards.

Industrial Insulation

Installation of thermal and acoustic insulation systems for industrial plants using mineral wool, expanded elastomer and insulated panels, in both civil and industrial environments.

Special Installations & Assembly

Elevated structural assembly, abnormal transport and heavy lifting operations from workshop fabrication through to final on-site installation.

Certifications

Our quality management system ensures compliance with the main European and international standards for metal construction and piping.

Accreditations

Our Certifications

ISO 9001

Quality Management System

ISO 9001 certification attests our commitment to systematic quality management: documented processes, internal audits, non-conformance management and continuous improvement across all production stages.

EN 1090

Steel Structure Execution — EXC3

CE marking for the execution of metal structures per EN 1090-1/-2. EXC3 class authorises us to fabricate structures for high-criticality applications including industrial plants, energy facilities and infrastructure.

EN ISO 3834

Welding Quality Requirements

Welding Quality System certification per EN ISO 3834-2 (Comprehensive requirements). Covers: procedure qualification (WPS/PQR), welder qualification (EN 9606), consumable control, traceability and NDT.

PED 2014/68

Pressure Equipment Directive

Compliance with PED Directive 2014/68/EU for design, manufacture and conformity assessment of pressure equipment (vessels, piping, accessories). Enables CE PED marking.

Quality & Compliance

Certifications

Quality is at the centre of every production process. We are currently in the process of obtaining ISO 9001:2015 and EN 9100:2018 certifications.

Quality Path

Certifications

Evolution Engineering & Services S.r.l. operates according to the highest international quality standards, with ISO 9001:2015 certification obtained and EN 9100:2018 certification in progress.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management System

ISO 9001:2015 defines the requirements for a quality management system oriented towards customer satisfaction and continuous improvement. The certification obtained structures and formalises our production, documentation and control processes in compliance with this international standard.

EN 9100:2018

Quality Management — Aerospace & Defence

EN 9100:2018 is the international standard for quality management systems in the aerospace, defence and related organisations sector. Based on ISO 9001, it introduces additional requirements specific to risk management, configuration and product safety. The certification in progress demonstrates our commitment to high-criticality sectors.

Our Approach

Quality as Daily Commitment

Regardless of the formal certification path, quality guides every stage of our work, from design to delivery.

Documented Processes

Every production phase is documented: control plans, work instructions, inspection records and full traceability of materials used.

Control & Inspection

Dimensional, visual and non-destructive testing (RT, UT, PT, MT, VT) carried out systematically during and at the end of every operation, with full reporting to the client.

Continuous Improvement

Non-conformance analysis, corrective actions and periodic process review. The goal is to eliminate error causes and constantly raise the quality level.

Qualified Personnel

Our technical team is trained and kept up to date on machining, welding and inspection techniques. Staff competence is a fundamental component of the quality system.

Material Traceability

Rigorous material management with origin documentation (EN 10204 certificates), batch traceability and compliance with project specifications for every order.

Customer Focus

Client satisfaction is our primary quality indicator. Listening to requirements, transparent communication and honouring commitments are the pillars of our approach.

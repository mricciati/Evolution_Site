What We Do

Our Services

Complete solutions for the heavy metalworking industry: from design to turnkey delivery.

🏗️

Steel Fabrication

We fabricate medium and large-scale steel structures for industrial, energy and infrastructure applications, fully compliant with EN 1090 European standards.

Our workshop handles carbon steels (S235–S690), stainless and special alloys, ensuring high dimensional tolerances and certified surface quality.

  • Load-bearing structures and composite beams
  • Pressure vessels and tanks
  • Structural racks and supports
  • Shells, dished ends and components
  • Gratings, walkways and industrial stairs
  • EN 1090 EXC1–EXC3 execution

Industrial Piping

We design and prefabricate complex piping systems for oil & gas, energy, chemical and pharmaceutical sectors. Every spoolpiece is tracked from material to final testing.

  • Carbon, stainless, duplex, P91/P92 piping
  • TIG, MIG, SAW, orbital welding
  • Isometrics and prefabrication from P&ID
  • Full NDT: RT, UT, PT, MT, VT
  • Hydrostatic / pneumatic pressure testing
  • ASME B31.1, B31.3, EN 13480 classes
📦

Skids & Prefabricated Modules

We build fully integrated process skids and prefabricated modules — including structure, piping, valves, instrumentation and electrical wiring — ready for on-site commissioning.

  • Process skids in steel, stainless, AISI 316L
  • GMP pharmaceutical modules (clean room)
  • Utility skids: steam, compressed air, water
  • Instrumentation and automation integration
  • Full FAT (Factory Acceptance Test)
  • IQ/OQ/PQ documentation for pharma

Executive Engineering

Our technical office manages the full design cycle: from reviewing client specifications to producing fabrication drawings and as-built documentation.

  • 3D modelling — SolidWorks, AutoCAD Plant 3D
  • Structural calculations (Eurocode, AISC)
  • Fabrication and as-built drawings
  • Bill of Materials (BOM) and purchase specs
  • Weld map and WPS/PQR
  • ITP (Inspection & Test Plan)
  • Project databook
🔑

Turnkey Supply

We manage the entire project: certified material procurement (EN 10204 3.1/3.2), workshop fabrication, testing, painting, packaging and shipping.

  • Dedicated project management
  • Certified material procurement
  • Workshop fabrication and quality control
  • Painting and surface protection
  • Logistics and abnormal transport
  • Complete documentation for client and authority

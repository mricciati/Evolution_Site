What We Do

Our Services

Heavy steel fabrication, industrial piping, naval sector, insulation and special installations: a complete offer for national and international heavy industry.

Heavy Steel Fabrication

We design and fabricate complex steel structures for industrial, energy and infrastructure applications. Our workshop handles carbon, stainless and special alloy steels with high dimensional tolerances and certified surface quality.

  • Load-bearing structures, composite beams and lattice towers
  • Pressure vessels and tanks
  • Hoppers, towers and special fabrications
  • Shells, dished ends and components
  • Hot-dip galvanising and C5-M industrial painting
  • EN 1090 EXC2 / EXC3 execution

Industrial Piping

We fabricate, supply and install industrial piping networks for process, energy and hospital plants. Every spoolpiece is tracked from material to final testing, with full WPS/PQR procedures and qualified personnel.

  • Carbon steel, stainless, duplex and HDPE piping
  • Certified TIG welding and submerged arc welding (SAW)
  • Radiographic (RT) and full NDT inspection
  • K-Flex and mineral wool insulation
  • Distribution manifolds and valving
  • Hospital, energy and process plant systems

Naval Sector

We supply specialist labour and fabricate structural components for the shipbuilding and port sector, with proven experience on contracts for the Italian Navy and major national shipyards.

  • Hull module assembly and welding
  • Loading ramps and naval steel structures
  • Qualified on-site labour supply
  • Naval steel and special alloy fabrication
  • Marine painting and surface treatment

Industrial Insulation

We design and install thermal and acoustic insulation systems for industrial plants and civil infrastructure, optimising energy efficiency and ensuring compliance with current regulations.

  • Pipe insulation with high-density mineral wool
  • Aluminium sheet cladding
  • REI fire-rated insulated panels
  • Expanded elastomer insulation (K-Flex)
  • Hospital, industrial and heat-recovery systems
  • REI 60/90/120 panel installation

Special Installations & Assembly

We manage elevated structural assembly, abnormal transport and heavy lifting operations, coordinating every phase from workshop fabrication to final on-site installation.

  • Elevated assembly with cranes and aerial work platforms
  • Abnormal transport on low-loader to port
  • Loading and sea shipment
  • Crane lifting up to 35T
  • Site safety coordination (D.Lgs. 81/08)
